#  CalibreViewer
9/30/2020

An app to show our collection of eBooks.

Connect your iPhone with a cable to a Mac.
Use The Mac's Finder to drag **metadata.db** into CalibreViewer in the Finder's window for the iPhone in the **Files** tab.
